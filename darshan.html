Darshan

One of the five elemental Shiva temples, the only one outside Tamil Nadu, this lovely shrine has many legends and histories. (See Wikipedia - Kalahasti Temple) It is the only reason for visiting Kalahasti and is located close to Tirupati.


The power there was tangible although I disliked the steel 'cattle pens' inside the temple which control the free darshan queues but mar the interior appearance and experience of darshan while waiting. Being hewn out of the rock itself the temple has the feeling of an ancient mysterious cave, adding to the powerful experience inside. Mobiles and cameras must be left outside in the market area.
