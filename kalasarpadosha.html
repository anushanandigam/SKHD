KAALA SARPA DOSHA PUJA


What is Kaala Sarpa Dosha?


When all the planets are placed in between Rahu and Ketu in a Horoscope, then a Kal Sarp Dosha or Yoga is formed.

The person having Kal Sarpa Dosha [Yoga] will have to face difficulties to lead a happy life. He/she may have problems in married life, Financial losses and invariant health problems, not getting children, Job problems and many more. The Kal Sarp Dosha can be cured by worshiping to Lord Shiva at some of the significant places of Mahadeva, Srikalahasti is one of them.

Here we give the details about Kaal Sarp Dosh Puja at Sri Kalahasti Uniqueness of Sri Kalahasteeswara Swamy at Sri Kalahasti Temple :

The Uniqueness of the Idol at Sree Kalahasthi Temple is that Lord Siva manifested here having all the 27 Nakshatras (stars) and 9 Rashis in his Kavacham.

Sri Kalahasti temple is the only temple which remains open during Grahanam (Solar and Lunar Eclipse) also.

Even Priests also never touches the Sivalingam here with hands.


Rahu Kethu Pooja / Sarpa dosha Puja


Rahu Ketu Sarpdosha Puja is performed in order to minimize the ill effects of this Dosha / yoga.

Thousands of devotees perform this Pooja in Srikalahasti and gets the expected results.


The Pooja in Srikalahasti:


The Rahu ketu Pooja is completed in 30 to 40 minutes, and it is will be fine if performed during Rahu kalam.


Kaal Sarp Dosh Puja Rates:


For Rs: 300/ This puja is performed in a hall out side the temple premises.

For Rs: 750/- pooja is performed near the main temple premises in an A/C hall which has Siva Sannidhi inside.


Do’s and Dont’s after performing the Kaal Sarp Dosha Puja Take a head bath before performing the pooja.


1. Females should perform the pooja before or after eight days of Menstruation.

2. Consult a good Astrologer and seek the guidance to perform the pooja according to your Janma Nakshathra.


Don’ts:


1.Do not visit Friends or Relatives house on the way back to your home after Rahu Ketu Pooja.

2. Pregnant ladies should not perform the Dosha Nivarana poojas in the temple. Avoid visiting temples where any type of Dosha Nivaran Pooja are performed.

3. Do not offer Sastanga Namaskara in any Naga Devatha temple.

4.Do not visit the Temple with oiled hair.
